My Projects


Pitted


     Pitted is a site I designed and built for one of my surfer buddies who happens to be stuck in Arizona, hundreds of miles from the ocean. He has built a community of people like him, who love to surf but live far from the beach. This site helps them share the locations of their surf spots, whether that be on a river, behind a boat, or in a pool.


I used: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, Mongoose, and more

SurfList


     Surflist is a place for people to buy and sell surfboards. The idea came about as a result of my frustration sorting through all of the junk that comes up when searching for something on Craigslist. This site is only for surfboards, so it's much easier to find what you want! And just like Craigslist, you don't need an account to sell something, just an email address!


I used: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, Mongoose, and more


Live      Repo

Sessions


     Sessions is a fun little app that combines info from several different sources to tell you where to grab a beer after you surf! You give it a surf spot, and it returns a map of nearby breweries, as well as a basic wave height surf forecast.


I used: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, and more
